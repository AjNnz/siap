About the Business

Isdang Presko ni
Lorena: Business and
Owner Profile

Isdang Presko ni Lorena is a well-established, local fish vending business in Poblacion, Cordova, Cebu, owned and operated by Lorena Cabataña. The business provides fresh, affordable fish sourced directly from local fishermen, serving both households buying for daily meals and small food businesses like carinderias.

Lorena, a 42-year-old single mother, has continued this family business for 27 years, with her five children also helping manage the operations. She represents a blend of traditional entrepreneurship and a readiness for modern methods.
